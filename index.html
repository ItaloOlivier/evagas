Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about EVAGas and our LPG services

What is EVAGas and what LPG services do you offer?

EVAGas is an authorised Oryx bulk LPG refilling supplier in South Africa. We offer comprehensive gas services including bulk LPG supply for industries and businesses, gas cylinder refilling (9kg, 19kg, 48kg sizes), wholesale distribution for resellers, and residential home delivery. We serve customers nationwide with competitive pricing and reliable service.

What areas in South Africa does EVAGas deliver to?

EVAGas provides nationwide LPG delivery across South Africa. We have strong coverage in Gauteng including Johannesburg, Pretoria, Centurion, and surrounding areas. Bulk deliveries can be arranged to any location in South Africa including Cape Town, Durban, Port Elizabeth, and other provinces.

Is EVAGas a legitimate and licensed gas supplier?

Yes, EVAGas is fully licensed and legitimate. We are an authorised Oryx LPG dealer and registered with the Liquefied Petroleum Gas Safety Association of Southern Africa (LPGSA). All our operations and technicians comply with SANS10087 safety standards and regulations.

What gas cylinder sizes can EVAGas refill?

EVAGas refills all standard LPG cylinder sizes including 9kg, 14kg, 19kg, and 48kg gas bottles. We perform comprehensive safety inspections on all cylinders before refilling and only fill cylinders that meet LPGSA certification standards. We offer both refill and exchange options.

How do I order bulk LPG gas from EVAGas?

Ordering bulk LPG from EVAGas is easy. You can call us directly at 010 599 2498, email sales2@congassa.co.za, or complete our online contact form. For bulk orders, we provide competitive pricing based on volume, flexible delivery scheduling, and dedicated account management for regular business customers.

Does EVAGas deliver gas cylinders to homes?

Yes, EVAGas provides residential home delivery for LPG gas cylinders. We deliver 9kg, 19kg, and 48kg cylinders directly to your doorstep. Our delivery team can also assist with safe installation and connection to your gas appliances. Contact us to schedule a convenient delivery time.
